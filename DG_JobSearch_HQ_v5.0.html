💡 v5 Strategy Note — Gov Portals vs Commercial Boards For EU markets (NL, DE, Nordics) you need no sponsorship — use gov portals mainly for English-job filters and employer discovery, not visa info. For UK always cross-reference DWP + Home Office Licensed Sponsors register before applying. For Gulf Dubai Careers and Hukoomi supplement Bayt/GulfTalent — not replace them. For Asia-Pacific gov portals are most valuable in Singapore (EP-flag) and Australia (482 sponsorship verification).
